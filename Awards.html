Junior Achievement 18 Under Eighteen Honoree

Leadership Recognition

February 6, 2024

I was selected as one of the Junior Achievement 18 Under Eighteen honorees after expressing qualities of a well-rounded leader within my school and community.

Recognition Highlights:

  • Selected from approximately 200 applicants to be one of the top 30 finalists.
  • Completed a rigorous 30-minute interview process discussing my leadership experiences, community service, and professional development.
  • Honored with one of only 18 final honoree positions.
  • Official award ceremony at Acrisure Stadium, where I networked with JA sponsors and public figures.

Supporting Achievements:

  • Women of Pittsburgh career highlight day—providing inspiration to 100+ girls annually.
  • Project Irreplaceable Breast Cancer Awareness writing campaign.
  • FIRST Robotics team participation and community STEM outreach.

Additional Recognition: Senator Devlin Robinson presented me with an award of recognition as one of the outstanding young citizens in Pennsylvania.

Reflection: This honor represents the culmination of my commitment to leadership, community service, and personal growth. It reinforced my belief that dedication and genuine engagement can create meaningful impact.

Outstanding Young Citizen

Trib Total Media Top 100

Recognition

I was recognized as one of the Top 100 Outstanding Young Citizens by Trib Total Media, highlighting my contributions to the Pittsburgh community and my commitment to STEM education and youth mentorship.

Recognition Focus:

  • Leadership and community involvement in Pittsburgh area.
  • Demonstrated commitment to making a positive impact in the community.
  • Dedication to STEM education and mentoring young women in engineering.
  • Professional development and continuous growth in technical fields.

Reflection: This recognition affirmed my passion for community engagement and demonstrated that authentic commitment to service and mentorship is noticed and valued. It motivated me to continue advocating for STEM education and supporting the next generation of engineers.

High School Graduation Speaker

Commencement Address

High School Graduation

I was selected to give the commencement address at my high school graduation—an honor I earned through dedication, hard work, and service to my school community.

The Opportunity:

  • Selected by administration to deliver the primary commencement address.
  • Spoke to graduating class, families, faculty, and community members.
  • Reflected on the high school journey and thanked mentors who supported my growth.
  • Provided encouragement and wisdom to fellow graduates.

Message Themes:

  • Gratitude to Chartiers Valley for shaping my educational and personal journey.
  • Recognition of teachers, counselors, and mentors who invested in student success.
  • Encouragement to embrace challenges and pursue authentic goals.
  • Vision for how graduates can create positive impact in their communities.

Reflection: Speaking at graduation was one of the most meaningful honors of my high school experience. It was a chance to give back to the community and faculty that shaped me, and to inspire my peers to pursue their passions with courage and integrity.
